FAQ

Clear answers before launch.

What is OvaSHA?

OvaSHA is a fixed-supply blockchain protocol focused on security, transparent execution, community and long-term value.

Which network is planned?

The initial deployment is planned for BNB Smart Chain, subject to final testnet validation and mainnet verification.

What is the total supply?

45,000,000 OVA.

Can additional OVA be minted?

The current protocol design does not include an unlimited minting mechanism.

How is the supply allocated?
  • DEX — 47%
  • CEX strategic reserve — 25%
  • Mining rewards — 6%
  • Presale — 5%
  • Emergency reserve — 5%
  • Marketing — 3%
  • Scheduled burns — 5%
  • Founder — 2%
  • Airdrop — 2%
When is the presale?

No date has been announced. The verified PinkSale pool will appear only through official channels.

Is OvaSHA audited?

Audit publication is part of phase two. No completed-audit claim is made until a final report is publicly available.

Will liquidity be locked?

The planned architecture includes liquidity locking. Final on-chain proof will be published at launch.

Where will the whitepaper be published?

On ovasha.com and the official GitHub organization. The current status is “publishing soon.”

Where are official announcements?

ovasha.com, @OvaSHA_Protocol on X and t.me/OvaSHAAnnouncements.
