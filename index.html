FAQ

Questions, answered clearly.

What is OvaSHA?

OvaSHA is a sustainable blockchain protocol focused on security, transparency, community and long-term value.

Why was OvaSHA created?

To build a blockchain ecosystem that prioritizes sustainability, accountability and long-term trust instead of short-term hype.

Which network will OVA launch on?

The initial deployment is planned for BNB Smart Chain, subject to final testing and deployment verification.

What is the total supply?

The total supply is fixed at 45,000,000 OVA.

Can additional OVA be minted?

No additional minting is planned. OVA is designed around a fixed-supply structure.

How is the supply allocated?
  • DEX Liquidity — 47%
  • CEX Strategic Reserve — 25%
  • Mining Rewards — 6%
  • Presale — 5%
  • Emergency Reserve — 5%
  • Marketing — 3%
  • Burns — 5%
  • Founder — 2%
  • Airdrop — 2%
Why are there scheduled burns?

The scheduled burns are designed to reduce total supply transparently over time, with planned burns in 2028 and 2030.

Will liquidity be locked?

Yes. Liquidity locking details will be disclosed before launch, together with verifiable on-chain information.

Will OvaSHA be audited?

Independent audit and public testing are planned before major launch milestones. No audit claim will be made before completion.

Will the source code be public?

The verified source code and relevant technical documentation are planned to be published through the official GitHub organization.

How are critical operations protected?

Critical protocol actions are designed around timelocks, multisig governance preparation and transparent reserve controls.

When is the presale?

The presale date has not yet been announced. Any official date will be shared only through verified OvaSHA channels.

Where are official announcements published?

Only through ovasha.com, the official X account, Telegram announcements and the official GitHub organization.

How can I contact the team?

You can contact the project through contact@ovasha.com.
